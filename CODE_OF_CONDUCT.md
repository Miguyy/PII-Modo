# Code of Conduct — Modo

This project adheres to the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) (version 2.1). The purpose of this Code of Conduct is to ensure a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for contributors, students, maintainers, and users of the Modo project.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to foster an open and harassment-free experience for everyone involved with Modo regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by mistakes, and learning from the experience.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Use of sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or educational setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Maintainers of the Modo project are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate, fair corrective action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all Modo project spaces, including GitHub issues and pull requests, project discussion channels, classroom or lab sessions where the project is used, and any other community channel where contributors represent the project.

## Reporting & Enforcement

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers. Preferred reporting channels:

- Open an issue in this repository labeled `code-of-conduct` (use the private report option if available), or
- Contact one of the project maintainers via their GitHub profiles listed in the README: Miguel Machado (https://github.com/Miguyy), Manuel Teixeira (https://github.com/Manutex78), or Linda Silva (https://github.com/LindaGlahy).

Reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. To protect privacy, maintainers will treat reports confidentially to the extent possible and will only share details with those necessary to investigate.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Project maintainers will follow these community-impact guidelines when determining consequences for violations:

1. **Correction** — Minor unprofessional behaviour. Consequence: private, written clarification and request to change behaviour.
2. **Warning** — Serious or repeated inappropriate behaviour. Consequence: formal warning and possible temporary restrictions.
3. **Temporary Ban** — Severe violations or clear pattern. Consequence: temporary removal of contribution privileges and access.
4. **Permanent Ban** — Demonstrating an ongoing pattern of violation or severe harassment. Consequence: permanent removal of access and privileges.
